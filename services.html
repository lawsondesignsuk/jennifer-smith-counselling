Pregnancy Loss

Grief, anxiety, depression, and PTSD are common and natural responses to baby loss, often leaving parents feeling overwhelmed and alone. Counselling can create a safe space for you to express your feelings of fear, anxiety, guilt, shame and anger, helping you live alongside your grief and feel more able to cope in the world.
